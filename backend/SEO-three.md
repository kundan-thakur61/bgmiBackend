```

- Explain why it matters

## Table of Contents
- Auto-generated from H2s

### [Subsection - H3]
Content with:
- Natural keyword usage (1-2% density)
- Practical examples
- Internal links (2-3 per section)
- Supporting images with alt text

## Key Takeaways
- 3-5 bullet points
- Include main keywords

## FAQ Section (H2)
### Question format - H3
Answer with 50-100 words

## Conclusion
- Summarize key points
- Call to action
- Internal link to related content

## References/Sources
[1] Source with URL
[2] Source with URL
```
